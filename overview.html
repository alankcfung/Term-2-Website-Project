A Overview

About

Clash Royale is a free-to-play mobile strategy game with around 120 million players worldwide. This video game was released March 2, 2016 and published by Supercell, the creaters of Clash of Clans. This game's objective is to destroy the opposing team's towers, with the collapse of the "King's Tower" being a instant win. Players are ranked by their number of trophies. Trophies are won or lost in 1v1 multiplayer battles. Players win a battle by destroying more towers than their oppenent. Players have a oppertunity to play with a 'trainer', where players can experiment new strategies on fun games they can be player with no such consquence anytime.

Fun Facts

The game was released globally on March 2, 2016. Clash Royale has then reached more than $2 billion in revenue in less than a year on the market. In 2017, Clash Royale by Supercell won the AMD eSports Audience Award.

Clans

Clash Royale includes the concept of clans, where a group of players combine together to form a group where they can donate or trade cards and play non-binding 1v1 and/or 2v2 battles together for fun.
